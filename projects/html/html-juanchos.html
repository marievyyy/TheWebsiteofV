Juancho's Burger Shop

The website is a group final project for the subject ELECTIVES 1 - Advanced Web Development. As a final project, each group needs to find a client that needs a website. Our group choose a client from a food industry and then search for a client. That's where we found Juancho's Burger Shop. We contacted the client and agreed to have a meeting with our group. After knowing their target audience and scope and limitations understanding what a client needs, we conducted our research or information gathering. Next is the web design process. The client also suggests a website that can serve as an inspiration for our design. Then we confirm the design with the client before moving on to any development. After finalizing the design we continue to the development stage. Here we keep out client informed on the status of the project. And the finally, launching the website and we presented our final project to our professor and to a panel of judges.
